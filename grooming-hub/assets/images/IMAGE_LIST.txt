**Background**: Clean, professional
**Style**: Dark, masculine, premium aesthetic

### Where to Get Images:

1. **Free Stock Photos**:
   - Unsplash.com (search: "men's grooming", "beard oil", "shaving")
   - Pexels.com (search: "male grooming", "razor", "beard care")
   - Pixabay.com (search: "shaving", "beard products")

2. **Product Photography**:
   - Take photos of actual grooming products
   - Use good lighting and clean background
   - Edit to match dark website theme

3. **E-commerce Sites**:
   - Reference product photography styles from:
     - The Art of Shaving
     - Beardbrand
     - Dollar Shave Club
   - Do NOT directly copy - use as inspiration only!
